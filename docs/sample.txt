Apple reported strong quarterly results with revenue growth driven by services and wearables. Management guided for stable gross margins next quarter, while noting currency headwinds. The company emphasized ongoing investment in AI features across the ecosystem and supply chain resilience.

NVIDIA continues to see high demand for data center GPUs, with backlog extending into next year. The firm discussed capacity expansions and software ecosystem growth. Analysts are watching for broader competition and potential normalization of pricing.

Microsoft highlighted cloud segment growth and operating expense discipline. Azure adoption in regulated industries was a key theme. Guidance suggested steady capex with AI infrastructure remaining a priority.
